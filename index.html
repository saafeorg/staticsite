SAAFE

We are members of the AAPI community that has been concerned with the rise of Anti-AAPI violence and abuse directed to the most vulnerable members of our community. We determined the best course of action to minimize this abuse towards the vulnerable members of our community was to create this SAAFEGUARD in order to allow them to continue their everyday activities as safely as possible and without fear.
